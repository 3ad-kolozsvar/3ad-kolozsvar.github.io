About us

We are the 3AD band from Cluj-Napoca. We started playing together in 2016, our members being Jenei Deborah (vocals), Ballai Álmos (guitar), Ádam Jakab (bass), and Ákos Jakab (drums).

Our style combines rock and roll and thrash metal: Thrash'n'roll. Our lyrics are written in English, our first original song, Stranger Angel, being released in the summer of 2018. Since then we released two more songs: In Real Life and Milk Overdose Under An Apple Tree.

Our first concert was during the Cluj Hungarian Cultural Days, since then we've played there 3 times. Our first concert in a night club was in March 2018 (the Flying Circus Club in Cluj), where we opened for the band Teddy Queen, with the help of Generáció Party. In June 2018 we performed on stage alongside the Loose Neckties Society band at the Apáczai Highschool. In October we played with the Metalon band at the La Țevi pub. In November, we were guest performers of Anna And The Barbies at /Form Space Cluj.

We kicked off 2019 very well, since we returned to /Form Space in January to open for the band Kistehén. In June, we won first place (music category) at the Artform Festival. We also got the chance to play at the Helló Ugar! festival in Ópusztaszer and the Szabadság Festival in Marghita.

A Stranger Angel, our band's first original song was written back in 2018. We consider it to be a big milestone in 3AD's history.
